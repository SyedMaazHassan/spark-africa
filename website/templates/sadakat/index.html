Sparks of Africa – An Insight

Sparks of Africa is a non-profit organization working primarily to establish sustainable empowerment programs for the young population in Nigeria.The youths of any country plays a key role in development and advancement of the country. According to the Nigerian Bureau of statistics, over 60% population is under 25 years of age.This invaluable asset of youth, provides a unique opportunity to positively influence the socio-economic growth of the nation.

This immense latent potential of youth can be harnessed by providing appropriate trainings and youth involvement in policy making. Our youth empowerment programs encompass a multi-pronged strategy focusing on providing opportunities and a facilitating environment for youth to transpire as agents of positive change. Sparks of Africa seeks to work with key stakeholders to ensure equal opportunities for all youngsters to participate and contribute to the socio-economic as well as political development processes for future.
